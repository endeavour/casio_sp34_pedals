5 pin header on my pcb:

Pin 1: 3.3v
Pin 2: P33 ESP32-S3 (GPIO 5) = left pedal
Pin 3: P34 ESP32-S3 (GPIO 6) = middle pedal
Pin 4: GND
Pin 5: P32 ESP32-S3 (GPIO 4 (ADC1_3)) = righ pedal

Readings unpressed:
left: 0
middle: 0
right: 4080ish, say above 4070 for unpressed

Readings pressed:
left: 1
middle: 1
right: fully depressed: 827. continuous scale.
